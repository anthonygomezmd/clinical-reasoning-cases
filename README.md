# Clinical Reasoning Cases

A collection of structured clinical cases designed for medical AI training and evaluation — built from real clinical experience across the U.S. and Mexico.

## Purpose

These cases are designed to help AI systems practice differential diagnosis, recognize clinical red flags, and generate complete, guideline-aligned responses. Each case reflects the complexity of real patient presentations, not just textbook examples.

## Case format

Each case includes:
- Chief complaint and HPI
- Relevant PMH, medications, and allergies
- Physical exam findings
- Differential diagnosis (broad → narrowed with reasoning)
- Expected AI response criteria
- Common reasoning errors to flag

## Specialties covered

Internal Medicine · Neurology · Pediatrics · Family Medicine · Emergency Medicine
